About

Vinnytsia is a picturesque city of Ukraine, which is located on the charming shores of the Southern Bug for over 600 years. This Podolsk region is famous for its clean rivers, mighty forests and fabulous meadows. There are two main versions of the origin of the name of the city. Some historians claim that the name comes from the word "wine", which translates from Old Slavic as "gift". Others - from the word "distillery", which means a place where beer is brewed. My Vinnytsia There are many historical and cultural monuments in Vinnytsia. And one of the unique historical architectural structures is the Jesuit monastery, built in 1610 on the donations of monks by nobles. This is a large complex of buildings with fortress "Walls". You can study the history and archeology of Vinnytsia in the Museum of Local Lore. My Vinnytsia The Transfiguration Monastery, built in 1616 by Paolo Fontana, has long been considered the city's calling card. This is the main Orthodox church in the city. My Vinnytsia The Arch at the entrance to the Central Park of Culture also deserves attention. M. Gorky. If you ever go there, you will have warm, unforgettable memories. My Vinnytsia My Vinnytsia Many tourists are attracted by Europe's largest floating fountain ROSHEN. This is an extremely beautiful work of art. Thanks to a special technology, the fountain winters under ice. My Vinnytsia Having visited the wonderful and friendly city of Vinnytsia, you will touch the centuries-old wisdom of our land.
